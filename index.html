My best neighborhood

The Lucky Town is a small town located in Seoul, the capital of South Korea. The Lucky Town is a literal name and the real name is Haengdong. When I was in college, my house was here and I was happy while living here. I made this website because I wanted many people to know this small town.

What's the point?

I made this website to recommend various stores located in Haengdong. Through the website, you can find out about the store's descriptions, contacts and operating hours. It will also be a reference to my brief comments on each store and visitors' reviews.
